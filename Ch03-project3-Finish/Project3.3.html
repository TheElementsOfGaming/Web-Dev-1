Mademoiselle Caroline Rivére

By Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Madomoiselle Caroline Rivére was painted in 1806 by French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hands in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm

Home: Musée du Louvre
